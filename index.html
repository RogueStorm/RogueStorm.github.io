What's the Story? All Leading to This.

After my wife had a stroke in 2011, her subsequent CASASIL diagnosis and my untimely redundancy in 2013, I have been on enforced retirement. I was required to look after my wife so I tried to get work from home roles. At the time, this proved to be very problematic. In light of Covid, this now seems a bit ironic. I have used some of this time to write a screenplay and a book. Head on over to the Books’ page to find out more.

What Next? Where do I go from here?

If I get enough interest, I will sell the book online through this site and possibly, through Amazon in paperback, book-reader and audio. The latter two are works in progress. I have been informed that in order to sell books, whether this is through self-publishing or through a traditional publisher, I need a social following. This site is a start and I’ll do my best to increase my social presence.
